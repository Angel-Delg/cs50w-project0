Rabat

The archaeological museum of Rabat, located near the great mosque, offers beautiful collections of Phoenician, Carthaginian and Roman remains, the latter coming from Volubilis and Chellah.

The gateway to Africa, as they call it, not only because it is separated from the European continent only by the Strait of Gibraltar, but also because of its cultural, economic and tourist predominance with respect to other African nations. And what is known about Morocco? Well, it is a very beautiful Arab country, and the gastronomy here is an art based on spices. However, is that all there is to know? Of course not.
